Solutions based on lsFusion

  • MyCompany

    Free and open source business management system. Suitable for wholesale, retail and online trading, services, contracts, small-scale and custom-made production. Easily scales and adapts to the needs of the business.

  • lsFusion ERP

    Trading system called "lsFusion ERP" Centralized solution for retail and wholesale automation.

  • MITE

    Cloud-based designer to make your own Industrial Internet of Things solution. Any complexity. Data in realtime. Easy to use.

  • barSettings

    The solution was developed by Settings on the basis of MyCompany to operate several catering companies in a single database with quantitative and summarized accounting of inventories in warehouses, support of recipes and production processes. BarSettings automates the reception and service of orders, both on-site and with delivery, integrates with the front office of the catering company and the online store

  • MyBalance

    Minimum set of tools for creating forms to replace spreadsheets in planning departments of enterprises. We offer a basic framework that allows by connecting modules to supplement the conditional "books" of spreadsheets with additional pages with the necessary functionality.
